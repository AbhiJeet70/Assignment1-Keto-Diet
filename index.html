Diet Plan

A keto diet is an eating plan that focuses on foods that provide a lot of healthful fats, adequate amounts of protein, and very few carbohydrates. The goal is to get more calories from fat than from carbs.

Fresh Vegetables

Non-starchy vegetables: broccoli, mushrooms, tomatoes, greens, and peppers

Fruits

Eat fresh fruits to consume natural sugar. It wil be a healthy way of intaking glucose.

Less Oily Foods

olive oil, coconut oil, avocado oil, sesame oil, and coconut butter can be eaten for fat consumption. But cooked food must be less oily.
